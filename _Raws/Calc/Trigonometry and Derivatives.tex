\section{Sine}

\begin{align*}
	f(x) = \sin(x)                \\
	-1 \leq        \sin(x) \leq 1 \\
	\sin(0) = 0                   \\
\end{align*}

For all integer multiples $\pi $, $\sin()$ attains 0

\[
	\sin(k\pi) = 0,  \text{ Where k is an integer}
\]

The graph of sine is periodic with a period of $2\pi$, meaning it repeats itself every interval of $2\pi$
